Sustainability Council

Our Sustainability Vision

Dextria's mission and vision relates to creating a better and healthy society. Our sustenability initiatives complemet the rounded development of that vision by proactively contributing to the larger social development and betterment of our community, country and the world. The three key pillars of a well rounded social development, we beleive are health, education and food. Our team is inspired to put in dedicated effort to help create a world free of hunger/malnutrition, better and healthy life and easy, affordable access to education.

Our business goals co-exist with our sustenability goals and our corporate policy revolves around leveraging our network and expertise to create an impact in improving education and health of our society as a whole. Equitable access to healthcare and education is a fundamental human right and is a cornerstone of social and economic development. With this belief, we partner with NGO's, Institutes and Forums to empower our sustenability initiatives.
